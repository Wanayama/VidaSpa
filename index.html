About Us

Our story

Providing quality service to every customer

Born and raised in Brazil I have always had a passion for beauty and health. Came to the US at the age of 22, and began working at a high-end salon, which heightened my passion for beauty. I decided to leave the salon and study esthetics to pursue my passion. In 2020 in the middle of the pandemic and with a lot of courage I decided to open up my own practice, and am living my dream making people confident, and happy with beautiful healthy skin, and giving them the knowledge to understand the needs of their skin, and that every skin can be perfect with the right treatment.
