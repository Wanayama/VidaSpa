Ultraformer MPT/ HIFU

High Intensity Focused Ultrasound is a non-surgical treatment that uses ultrasound to lift, firm and tighten the skin. HIFU deposits focused ultrasound energy below the surface of the skin, enabling all the layer of skin to contract in just one treatment! This stimulates the creation of new collagen in the area; therefore this treatment is highly effective for firming, tightening and liftin the facial skin and can reduce excess skin on the body too!
