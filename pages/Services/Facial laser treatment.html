Carbon Peel / Hollywood Peel

Carbon laser peel isn’t a traditional chemical peel. Instead, your
esthetician uses a carbon solution and lasers to create a peeling
effect. The lasers don’t penetrate the skin too deeply, so there's
very little recovery time. The treatment takes about 30 minutes,
and you can resume regular activity right away. Minimally invasive
skin resurfacing treatments are very popular for improving the
appearance of skin What Does a Carbon Laser Peel Treat?
The carbon laser peel is effective for treating acne. The whole process
works to eliminate oil, debris, and bacteria in the pores, which are
often causes of acne. Doctors find that it can clear up existing acne
and prevent new breakouts from occurring for weeks or months.‌
You may need multiple treatments to see effects on acne. The
treatment is non-irritating, so it doesn’t make inflamed skin feel
worse during treatment. You can repeat carbon laser peels as
necessary without negative side effects.‌‌
In addition to treating acne, carbon laser peels may also improve: Signs
of premature aging, Dull complexion, tone, and texture, Fine lines and
wrinkles, Oily skin, Dark spots, Mild acne scars

Genesis Laser

Laser Genesis treatment is a safe and effective treatment for those
battling against acne, acne scars, signs of aging, and other imperfections
. As a result, your active acne will diminish, future breakouts will be
prevented, scars will heal, and the appearance of your wrinkles and creases will improve.
HOW LASER GENESIS TREATMENT WORKS
Laser Genesis is a non-invasive, non-ablative treatment that’s scientifically
proven to improve imperfections such as acne, acne scarring, age spots, and
reduce pore size. It works not only in the face but also in the neck, back,
and other parts of the body. Laser Genesis works effectively by using the
heat from a laser to kill bacteria on the epidermis and deep within the skin.
It also stimulates collagen growth. Treatment is followed by Carboxy therapy
for enhancement of results and overall skin quality.

Laser Skin Brightening

Our amazingly powerful Melasma Laser focuses on lightening pigmentation and also
the suppression and re-education of the melanocytes (cells responsible for
melanin production).
Treating melasma is a gradual process that requires multiple sessions. Typically
it takes several treatments for best results. Since melasma is a chronic skin
disorder, maintenance therapy is needed to control the production of melanin
and strict avoidance of sun exposure. Treatment is followed by Carboxy
therapy for enhancement of results and overall skin quality.
